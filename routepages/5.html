Overview

Route 5 provides a unique route stretching East and South down Lemay from the DTC before looping at the Foothills Mall.

The route was one of the early routes released in the first decade after Transfort's 1974 launch. While it initally only served community members east of the DTC, it was gradually extended to stretch further south and further east.

The route is preserved in the optimized plan as a core route, which will be running every 30 minutes during an 8 hour peak and every hour for the other 6 hours.
