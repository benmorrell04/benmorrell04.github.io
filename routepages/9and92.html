Overview

The 9 and 92 each stretch west along Laporte, with the former looping up Oerland to return on Vine and the latter curving at Poudre High School.

The 92 only runs once per day after 4 pm to serve PHS students. It is not intended to replace the school's busing system, but to provide an alternative for some students to return home.

Neither of these routes will be continued in the optimized transit plan. In spite of apparent demand, the ridership was too low to maintain. They have, however, indicated future plans of reinstating route 9 service.

By Ben Morrell 2025
