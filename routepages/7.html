Overview

The 7 Route runs southeast along Shields and Drake from the Campus Transit Center. It serves a unique segment east of College on Drake.

It was an early addition to the network, originally connecting all the way down to Harmony where the Hewlett-Packard plant once was. It was moved north as southern transit coverage improved.

In the new plan, the route will lean into its unique Drake coverage; in fact, it will be the only route to run along Drake in any capacity. It's connection to the CTC will be removed, likely intending for passengers to use the MAX route as a connection.
