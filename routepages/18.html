Overview

The 18 bus serves a crucial portion of Fort Collins, running southeast from the Dowtown Transit Center to eventually curve on eastern Prospect just past Timberline.

It will be heavily altered in the new plan, to the extent that it's been labeled the 18N. After its existing loop, it will turn left onto Timberline and run south along the street. After it intersects with the 16, it will come back, but future plans see it extend all the way down to Trilby and up to the South Transit Center.

By Ben Morrell 2025
