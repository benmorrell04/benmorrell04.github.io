Overview

Route 11 used to connect the South Transit Center to a path along Horsetooth from Taft Hill to Ziegler, where it turned into the 12.

It was suspended alongside the 12 in 2021 due to a shortage of new bus drivers. The routes were chosen to be cut due to low ridership.

The route will not be reintroduced in the optimized transit plan, leaving Harmony empty west of Shields.

The original 11 route was Transfort's busiest, running out of the Campus Transit Center before it was replaced by the 32 and its label was used for a southern route.

By Ben Morrell 2025
