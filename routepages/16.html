Overview

Route 16 covers the Eastern portion of Harmony from the South Transit Center to the Harmony Transfer Center just before I25. It connects this southeastern corner of Fort Collins to the MAX BRT route and a variety of businesses.

The importance of this route to the MAX caused it to be brought over into the optimized transit plan. The route will largely stay the same, though it will no longer return by going straight along Harmony, instead curving to Rock Creek and Fossile Ridge High School both ways.
