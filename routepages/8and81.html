Overview

The 8 and 81 currently have identical path stretching northeast from the DTC. The 8 runs straight through College on Willow before curving north along Linden, Redwood, and Blue Spruce and then back south along college. The 81 turns left on college, stopping at the stame stops in reverse.

In the new plan, the routes will no longer intersect. They will still run to College and Willow, but the 8 will now turn left on the College intersection and flip around on Willox whereas the 81 will continue straight to cover the eastern curve and north to connect to the Poudre Valley Mobile Home Park.

By Ben Morrell 2025
