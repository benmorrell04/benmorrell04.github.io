Overview

Launched in May 2014, the MAX is Fort Collins' first bus rapid transit system. It runs between the North and South transit center, connecting them and anything in between. This includes the Colorado State University campus, foothills mall, and many employers; around 60% of Fort Collins jobs are located within one mile of the Max corridor.

The route costed $87 million, $48.9 million of which coming from construction. $69.5 million (80%) of which was funded by the Federal Transit Administration and the rest by these local sources: the Colorado Department of Transportation, CSU, the Downtown Development Authority, and the city of Fort Collins.
